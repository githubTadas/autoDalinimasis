Step by step guide

1. Find the car and get the keys

We'll send you the car's exact location and a code to open its lockbox 15 minutes before your trip begins.

2. Take photos and check for damage

Your photos protect you from being held liable for damage you didn't cause.

3. Refuel for free

The cost of fuel is included with your distance charge - just use the fuel card or submit a receipt.

4. Clean up and park

Return the car according to its parking instructions and make sure it's as clean as it was before your trip.

5. Check, lock and leave

Take photos again, lock the car, put the key back in the lockbox, and that's it - you're done!

Why borrow cars with Car Next Door?

Free membership

Join the community for free and pay only when you need a car. Borrow by the hour or by the day, with fuel and insurance included.

Cars on your street

With over 1,300 share cars across Australia, you can borrow a car close to home – sign up to see the exact locations of cars on our fleet map.

Save money

Your neighbour’s car can be up to 50% cheaper than a traditional rental, plus you only pay for the time you need it.

Comprehensive insurance included

You’re covered if you have an accident during your trip. You can even reduce your Damage Cover Liability for more peace of mind, just like a rental car.

24 hour support

All bookings come with 24-hour roadside assistance and member support. We’ve got instructions for every step of the way in our support centre.

Help the community and the environment

When you book with us, the majority of your money goes back to your community, you’re making use of unused cars, and we offset the CO 2 of every km you drive.
